# Quantum Circuit Born Machines vs. Generative Adversarial Networks: A Comparative Study on Low-Dimensional Generative Modelling 

This project serves as a proof-of-principle study, aiming to investigate the potential of quantum advantage. It compares the performance of a classical Wasserstein Generative Adversarial Network (WGAN) and Quantum Circuit Born Machine (QCBM). They are compared in terms of quality of the pattern generation using metrics such as KL-divergence. The dataset used for training and evalutaion is bars_and_stripes.py. It consists of 14 patterns of size 3x3, formed by vertical and horizontal lines.
